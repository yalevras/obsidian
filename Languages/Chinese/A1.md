Negating verbs and adverbs with 不

他们不是坏孩子。
They are not bad kids

我哥哥不高，但是很帅。
My older brother is not tall, but he is very handsome

坏 huài - bad
孩子 háizi - child
喝 hē - to drink
酒 jiǔ - alcohol
想 xiǎng - to want/to think (about), to desire
工作 gōngzuò - to work
喜欢 xǐhuan - to like, to be fond of
为什么 wèishénme - why
咖啡 kāfēi - coffee
时间 shíjiān - time
饿 è - hungry
贵 guì - expensive
这 zhè - this
公司 gōngsī - the company
老板 lǎobǎn - the boss
高 gāo - tall
但是 dànshì - but
帅 shuài - handsome
哥哥 gēge - older brother
