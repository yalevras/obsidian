
Greek nouns:
masculine - αρσενικό
preceded by ο, usually end in ος, ης, or ας

feminine - θηλυκό
preceded by η, usually end in a η or ις

neuter - ουδέτερο
preceded by το, usually end in ο ι or μα
se (ιν, ον, το, ατ) combines with το to make στο
