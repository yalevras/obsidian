
Greek nouns:
masculine - αρσενικό
preceded by o, usually end in os, hs, or as

feminine - θηλυκό
preceded by h, usually end in a h or is

neuter - ουδέτερο
preceded by to, usually end in o i or ma
se (in, on, to, at) combines with to to make sto
