Lesson 2.3
だ declares something is a certain way. It attached to nouns and な-adjectives
- きれいだ／綺麗だ
- 学生だ

Lesson 2.4
じゃない is the negative counterpart of だ
- きれいじゃない／綺麗じゃない
- 学生じゃない

Lesson 2.5
Particles are called じょし／助詞
Uses of が
	1. Marks the subject of a sentence (subject performs an action or exhibits a state)
		彼女が学生だ？
	2. Used with questions words like 誰 or 何. が marks the subject unknown to the listener.
		誰が先生だ？
	3. If you respond to the above question using が, it is being used to specify who the teacher is and, at the same time, that the other people are not teachers and only this teacher is relevant to the conversation.
